The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina

Today, Sabrina has taken a more mature turn in the entertainment scene. A new horror comic series named Afterlife With Archie left readers wanting more of the raw power that is the character of Sabrina. She soon earned her own series because of this.

The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina arrived in 2014, which showcased the origin story of the character. It is set in the 1960s, and begins with Sabrina being given away to her aunts at birth.
